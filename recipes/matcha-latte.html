Matcha Latte

Description

The history of matcha can be traced back to the Tang Dynasty in China. Today, nearly a thousand years later, matcha is still enjoyed by many. Matcha is known for its earthy, somewhat bitter flavor as well as its bright green color.

"This recipe for a hot matcha latte is simple and delicious!" -Tiffany H.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons matcha powder
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 3/4 cup hot milk

Steps

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a mug.
  2. Add the hot water and whisk up until no lumps remain.
  3. Stir in the honey.
  4. Add the milk and whisk to combine.
